# Sales Rep Quick Start — Interactive Deck

## 🎯 5-Minute Setup Before Your First Call

### Step 1: Open the Deck (2 min before call)
```
http://localhost:3001/deck/test-001?tier=A
```

Replace `tier=A` with:
- **`tier=A`** - Tier A guarantee (Top 3 in 90 days)
- **`tier=B`** - Tier B soft guarantee
- **`tier=C`** - Tier C Premium (no guarantee)

### Step 2: Go Fullscreen
- **Mac:** `⌘ + Ctrl + F`
- **Windows:** `F11`

### Step 3: Test Navigation
- Press `→` arrow a few times
- Press `←` to go back
- Press `Home` to return to start

### Step 4: Minimize Until Call
- Keep tab open
- When Zoom call starts, share **browser window** (not entire desktop)

## ⌨️ During the Call - Keyboard Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `→` or `Spacebar` | Next slide |
| `←` | Previous slide |
| `Home` | Jump to first slide |
| `End` | Jump to last slide |

## 📊 The 11-Slide Flow

| # | Slide | Time | What You Say |
|---|-------|------|--------------|
| 1 | **Hook** | 30s | "Is Google Maps stealing or sending you money?" |
| 2 | **Maps Pack** | 1m | "See these 3 businesses? They're getting the calls right now." |
| 3 | **Money Math** | 1m | "You've got 34 reviews. Your competitor has 187. That gap = $15,750/month lost." |
| 4 | **Phase 1** | 2m | "90-Day Sprint: overhaul, citations, review surge, call tracking." |
| 5 | **Phase 2** | 2m | "Monthly Autopilot: 3 posts/week, review monitoring, Money Sheet." |
| 6 | **Proof** | 1m | Case study or market data proof point |
| 7 | **Guarantee** | 1m | "Top 3 in 90 days or we work free for 6 months." (if Tier A) |
| 8 | **Territory** | 30s | "We only take one shop per 5-mile radius. Your spot is open now." |
| 9 | **Pricing** | 2m | "Two ways to start: Box A or Box B..." **→ STATE PRICE → SHUT UP 8 SECONDS** |
| 10 | **Next 7 Days** | 1m | "When you say yes: grab card, lock territory, posts live in 7 days." |
| 11 | **End** | 10s | Logo + CTA |

## 🎬 Critical Moments - Where Sales Happen

### Slide 3: Money Math
**DO:**
- Let the numbers count up (don't rush to next slide)
- Pause after "$15,750/month" appears
- Wait 3 seconds before saying anything

**DON'T:**
- Talk over the animation
- Skip this slide
- Minimize the impact with "well, it's an estimate..."

### Slide 9: Pricing
**DO:**
- Read Box A first: "Month-to-month: $1,500 setup + $500/month"
- Read Box B second: "Or waive the setup, $500/month × 12 months"
- Say: "Most shops choose Box B. Which works better for you?"
- **HIT SPACEBAR**
- **COUNT TO 8 IN YOUR HEAD**
- **SHUT UP**

**DON'T:**
- Keep talking after stating price
- Offer discounts
- Say "does that make sense?"
- Fill the silence

## 🚨 Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Talking too fast | Pause 2 seconds after each slide transition |
| Skipping Slide 3 animation | Wait for "$15,750" to finish counting |
| Breaking silence after price | Count to 8. First person to talk loses. |
| Not using keyboard nav | Practice 5 times before first call |
| Fumbling at pricing slide | Have your 2-box speech memorized |

## 🆘 Emergency Help

### Deck won't load
```bash
# In Terminal:
cd ~/maps-autopilot/sales-deck-app
npm run dev
```
Open http://localhost:3001

### Wrong guarantee showing
Check URL parameter:
- Tier A market → `?tier=A`
- Tier B market → `?tier=B`
- Tier C market → `?tier=C`

### Animations frozen
- Refresh page (Cmd+R)
- If still broken, check console (Cmd+Option+I)

### Can't navigate slides
- Click anywhere on the page first (give it focus)
- Try arrow keys again
